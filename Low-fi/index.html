Teenage and youth suicide is one of the major causes of unnatural deaths. We want to bring the problem to light, and provide some resources for people who encounter peers with suicidal thoughts. We would also provide some information on suicides and the types of suicides.

Please take time to read through the articles to gain a better understanding of the situation and know what you can do. Feel free to download our online e-brochures to distribute them amongst your peers.
